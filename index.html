My Work (Connecting Inspiration)

I build and scale recommendation systems at places like YouTube and Pinterest. I genuinely love working on tech where the end goal is human: helping people discover creative ideas, recipes, hobbies, and video clips that spark real-world inspiration.

Roots & Perspective

I spent my first 17 years in Kerala, a uniquely progressive state in India known for its natural beauty & deep religious harmony. Since then, I’ve moved across multiple cities in India and the US. Constantly adapting to new environments gave me an incredibly open mind, while my natural curiosity and analytical drive keep me grounded. To me, the real foundation of compatibility is being able to enjoy the simple, everyday moments together, whether that means falling down a great YouTube rabbit hole on history and philosophy, or just sharing whatever is currently playing in our headphones.

The Balance

I approach training with intense discipline, pushing my limits in the gym. I also enjoy the simple routine of prepping healthy food to sustain it all. But outside of that, I’m highly relaxed. I love being a generalist who knows bits and pieces about a lot of subjects. In fact, I was on many quiz teams in my school and college years.

Creative Outlets & Soundtracks
